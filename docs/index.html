Why Vulnerability MTTR Alone Misleads

Add MOVA to measure real backlog risk.

MTTR shows how fast work closes. MOVA shows how old the open backlog is today.

The paradox is simple: MTTR can rise while backlog age falls. When teams finally close older findings, the age of closed work goes up. MTTR can look worse even while the remaining backlog gets healthier.

Flow

Backlog age

MOVA

How old is the work still open today?

MTTR is useful. The mistake is letting it decide alone.

What MOVA reveals

The backlog story MTTR can miss

  • Whether older backlog is actually shrinking
  • Whether recent closures are reaching the aging tail
  • Whether low MTTR is masking stranded findings

What to report

Use a small metric set

  • MTTR for closure flow
  • MOVA for open backlog age
  • Open count for backlog size
  • 180+ days for the aging tail

Takeaway

Read the system, not just the metric.

Do not let low MTTR outrank backlog age. Use MTTR and MOVA together to see whether the system is getting healthier.
